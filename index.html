Front-End Development

Front-End Architecture

We've always paid close attention to proper architecture. In a field where many possible solutions to a problem exist, we should always prefer the simplest one. Adapting a solid architectural foundation with agile principles could be regarded as a contradiction, which we happily offer as our skillset.

services 3/4

Consulting

We can offer a wide range of consulting services in the area of front-end development and architecture. Sometimes a new external view on some topics is enough to get the ball rolling. We specialize in consulting services that help to set the right course in critical phases of client projects.

services 4/4

User-Centered Design

Even the best possible development job would be utterly pointless if it's not solving the users' most pressing problems. We believe that user-centred design is equally important as development and therefore embrace many UCD methods and concepts in our overall process. We assist you in creating user journey maps, user research and agile usability testing.
